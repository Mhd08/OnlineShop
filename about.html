Welcome to Shoura's Bakery

Located in the heart of Beirut, Lebanon, Shoura's Bakery is a family-owned haven for artisanal baked goods. With a deep love for traditional Lebanese flavors and a passion for innovation, we bring you freshly baked breads, pastries, and cakes that warm the heart and soul.
For over 20 years, Shoura's Bakery has been a cornerstone of the Beirut community, blending heritage recipes with modern techniques to create treats that are as beautiful as they are delicious. From the aroma of freshly baked saj bread to the sweetness of our signature maamoul, every item is crafted with care and dedication.
Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a comforting bite, we invite you to savor the flavors of Lebanon with us. At Shoura's Bakery, every bite tells a story of love, tradition, and excellence.
